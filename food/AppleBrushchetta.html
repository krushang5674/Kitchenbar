Apple Bruschetta


Total Time:10 min
Prep:10 min
Difficulty:Easy
Course:Sweet Dish


Ingredients

*1/4 cup (60 mL) olive oil
*2 Tbsp. (30 mL) cider vinegar
*2 Tbsp. (30 mL) apple jelly
*3 Tbsp. (45 mL) chopped fresh coriander
*3 garlic cloves, minced Salt and pepper to taste
*1 cortland apple unpeeled and diced
*1 Granny Smith apple unpeeled and diced
*8 plum tomatoes seeded and diced
*1 sweet green pepper diced
*5 chopped scallions
*1 sliced and toasted baguette

Instruction

In a small bowl, mix dressing ingredients together.
Set aside.
In a large bowl, mix vegetables and apples together.
Pour dressing over them, stirring to coat everything, and let stand at least 2 hours at room temperature.
Serve on toasted slices of baguette.
