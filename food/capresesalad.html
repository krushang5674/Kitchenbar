Caprese Salad



Total Time: 10 min
Prep: 10 min
Difficulty: Easy
Course: Side Dish


Ingredients

* 3 vine-ripe tomatoes, 1/4-inch thick slices
* 1 pound fresh mozzarella, 1/4-inch thick slices
* 20 to 30 leaves (about 1 bunch) fresh basil
* Extra-virgin olive oil, for drizzling
* Coarse salt and pepper


Instruction

In a small saucepan, bring balsamic vinegar to a boil over medium-low heat.
Cook for 10 to 20 minutes, or until balsamic has reduced to a thicker glaze.
Remove from heat and transfer to a bowl or cruet. Allow to cool.
When you're ready to serve, arrange tomato and mozzarella slices on a platter.
Arrange basil leaves between the slices. Drizzle olive oil over the top of the salad, getting a little bit on each slice.
Do the same with the balsamic reduction, making designs if you want.
Store extra balsamic reduction in fridge for a later use.
End with a sprinkling of kosher salt and black pepper. Serve as a lunch, with crusty bread.
Or serve alongside a chicken main course for dinner.
